Committed METS are reserved METS locked to selected features and cannot be used anywhere.
What are Un-Allocated METS?
Unallocated METS are flexible credits that are not locked and can be used across all supported features.
What are Complementary Credits?
Complementary credits are free credits provided to accounts with feature-based selection.
What are Testing Credits?
Testing credits are specifically allocated for testing purposes and sandbox usage.

Channel-level patterns and billing accuracy signals

These channels show clean billing with no reversals across all three months — consumption is fully attributed and no credits were issued.
